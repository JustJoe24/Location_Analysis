How the score works

Every station gets a score from 0 (weakest) to 1 (strongest). Each activity signal is normalized against the busiest station, then combined:

  • 35% lunch-hour trips (11 am–3 pm)
  • 25% total trips
  • 20% weekday trips
  • 10% lunch-hour share of all trips
  • 10% casual (non-member) trips

Markers are numbered by rank, so score is never conveyed by color alone.

How the score works

Each station's activity is normalized against the busiest station, then combined:

  • 35% lunch-hour trips (11am–2pm)
  • 25% total trips
  • 20% weekday trips
  • 10% lunch-hour share of all trips
  • 10% casual (non-member) trips
